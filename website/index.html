🎮 Pixel Fighters

A Super Smash Bros-style 2D fighting game built with Python & Pygame

Play Now in Your Browser!

Loading may take a few moments. The game requires a keyboard to play.

Features

🎯 2-Player Battle

Local multiplayer action - grab a friend and fight!

🎭 4 Unique Characters

Choose from Mask Dude, Ninja Frog, Pink Man, or Virtual Guy

⚔️ Physics-Based Combat

Realistic knockback that scales with damage percentage

🎪 3 Unique Stages

Fight across different platform layouts with traps and hazards

💥 Traps & Hazards

Dodge fire, spike heads, and falling rock heads

🍎 Power-ups

Collect fruits for temporary double damage

🔊 Sound & Music

Immersive audio with sound effects and background music

✨ Smooth Animations

Fluid character animations for all actions

Controls

Player 1 (Red)

  • A / D - Move left/right
  • W - Jump (press again in air for double jump)
  • F - Attack

Player 2 (Blue)

  • ← / → - Move left/right
  • ↑ - Jump (press again in air for double jump)
  • , - Attack

ESC - Return to character selection

How to Play

Objective

Knock your opponent off the stage to score a KO! Each player has 3 lives.

Damage System

Each hit increases your opponent's damage percentage. Higher damage means stronger knockback when hit - just like Super Smash Bros!

Hazards

Fire: Deals 10% damage on contact
Spike Heads: Instant death - lose a life!
Rock Heads: Falling hazards that kill on impact

Power-ups

Collect falling fruits to gain double damage for 10 seconds! Use this time to deal massive knockback.

About

Pixel Fighters is a passion project built with Python and Pygame, inspired by the Super Smash Bros series. It features physics-based combat, multiple characters and stages, and a complete sound system.

The game is open source and available for anyone to play, modify, and learn from!
